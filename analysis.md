# Credit Score Analysis

## Score Distribution
- 0-100: Risky/Exploiting users
- 100-300: Frequent liquidation, irregular repayments
- 300-700: Moderate users
- 700-900: Responsible users
- 900-1000: Ideal behavior

## Observations
- Users with high deposit/repay ratio tend to be responsible.
- Liquidation events are major negative indicators.
